Methodology note. Illustrative absenteeism pilot, 3 sites, 6,000 employees (2,000 per site), shown as a pre-pilot model. Exposure assumes 12 absence days / employee / year (SA published average) at R1,100 / absent day; both are configurable inputs the pilot replaces with client actuals once HR and screening data connects. Risk scoring: WHtR-based clinical model with mining multiplier (1.30), banded Low/Medium/High/Critical. Saving projections apply an illustrative published benchmark (15-25%, shown at a conservative 20%) for absence reduction in high-risk populations under structured chronic-care intervention. These figures are modelled projections, not measured outcomes; a pilot validates them against the client's own data.
